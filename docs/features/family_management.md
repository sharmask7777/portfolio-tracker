# Family Management

## Tracking Portfolios for Multiple Family Members
To simplify wealth management for households, the tracker allows for the consolidation of multiple individual portfolios under a single family view.

## Managed Profiles and Family Groups
- **Managed Profiles:** Primary users can create and manage profiles for dependents or other family members, keeping their PAN-level CAS imports separate.
- **Family Groups:** Profiles can be grouped together to view aggregate net worth, family-level X-Ray analytics, and combined tax liabilities.
