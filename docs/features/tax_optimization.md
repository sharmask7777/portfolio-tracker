# Tax Optimization

## Advanced Tax Engine Features
Our tax engine is specifically tuned for Indian taxation rules, offering detailed breakdown and optimization strategies for realized and unrealized capital gains.

## STCG vs LTCG Tracking
The tracker accurately separates Short-Term Capital Gains (STCG) from Long-Term Capital Gains (LTCG) based on asset class holding periods.

## Indian Taxation Rules
- **Budget 2024 Updates:** Incorporates the latest changes in taxation rates for equity and debt instruments introduced in the 2024 Budget.
- **Grandfathering:**
  - *Pre-2018 Equity:* Implements the grandfathering clause for equity investments made on or before January 31, 2018.
  - *Pre-2023 Debt:* Accounts for the grandfathering of debt mutual funds purchased before April 1, 2023, retaining their indexation benefits where applicable.
