# Analytics & X-Ray

## Deep X-Ray Capabilities
The Portfolio Tracker provides deep "X-Ray" analytics to give investors a granular view of their holdings.

## Features
- **Expense Ratios:** Aggregates and calculates the weighted average expense ratio of the entire portfolio.
- **Portfolio Overlap:** Identifies overlapping underlying stocks across different mutual fund schemes to expose hidden concentration risks.
- **Sector Intersections:** Analyzes the sector allocation across all funds (e.g., Financials, Technology, Healthcare) to give a macro view of the portfolio's sectoral bias.
